Proposed PCM-LAMP At-Home Genomic System

Low-Resource Isothermal Molecular Detection Platform

1. PCM Heating Unit

Core Technology: Modified Paraffin Wax Phase-Change Material (PCM)
  • Thermal Target: Steady-state transition at 60-65°C for >60 minutes.
  • Visual Indicator: Clear top allows observation of "cloudy liquid" state for reaction ready-signaling.
  • Interface: Slot lined with thermally conductive plastic or aluminum for optimized heat transfer to reaction tube.
  • Safety: Insulated outer/bottom layers to prevent user contact with transition temperatures.

Note: Device is optimized for stove-top boiling.

2. Reaction Tube & Chemistry

  • Dual-Chamber Design: Lysis buffer and lyophilized beads separated by an impermeable membrane.
  • Activation: Secondary screw-cap with internal puncture pin allows for a sealed-system fluid transfer.
  • Chemistry: Warm-start colorimetric LAMP using metal-ion indicators (non-pH dependent).
  • Contamination Control: Permanent seal design; tube remains closed post-amplification to contain amplicons. Double bag provided for additional security. Contamination risk of amplicons is primarily its potential to affect future test results.

3. Operational Procedure

Step Action Duration
1. Sample Cheek swab into lysis chamber. expected to take 5-10 min
2. Activate Screw puncture cap to drop lysis onto bead. instant
3. Incubate Insert via heat-resistant handle atop the puncture cap into PCM unit. expected to take 20-40 min

4. Result Interpretation

Results are determined by color change observed through the clear reaction tube immediately upon removal:

Negative
Yellow
Positive
Pink / Magenta
